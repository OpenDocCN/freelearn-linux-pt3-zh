Section 2: Best Practices for Linux Technologies

The objective of Section 2 is to introduce you to technical best practices and a high-level understanding of the workings of difficult technological concepts that are core to the system administration function. We will investigate design elements and architectures rarely covered but critical to Linux.

This part of the book comprises the following chapters:

  • Chapter 3, System Storage Best Practices
  • Chapter 4, Designing System Deployment Architectures
  • Chapter 5, Patch Management Strategies
  • Chapter 6, Databases
